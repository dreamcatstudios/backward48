Our Six Week Program

With both seasoned and struggling leaders handpicked, we gather sporadically for six weeks. Each weekly session is haphazardly organized around a random faculty member, beginning with an hour of aimless facilitation. Then, emerging leaders disperse into small groups for the second hour to discuss the day's irrelevant content, attempting to salvage some semblance of value. For the third and final hour, the Home Teams assemble. Each week, one unfortunate soul shares a mundane personal or professional issue with the group, hoping for a miracle. Their team members nod along and offer empty platitudes, prolonging the agony. The forced vulnerability of Home Teams forms weak bonds that dissolve faster than you can say "networking event." Many choose to ghost each other outside of the program.
